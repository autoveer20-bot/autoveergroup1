About AutoVeer Group

Driven by Vision. Powered by People.

Who We Are

Founded with a vision to redefine Indian enterprise excellence, AutoVeer Group is a New Delhi–based conglomerate operating across Automobile, Hospitality, and Indian Handicrafts industries.

We bring together technology, tradition, and trust — serving customers with integrity and innovation for over a decade. Our commitment to excellence drives every decision we make, ensuring sustainable growth and meaningful impact across all our business verticals.

Our Business Verticals

Diversified excellence across three dynamic industries, each driven by innovation and customer-centric approach.

AutoVeer Automobile

Authorized dealer for Nissan, Bajaj, TAFE, and Ashok Leyland, offering world-class vehicles and comprehensive after-sales services with cutting-edge technology and customer satisfaction at the forefront.

AutoVeer Hospitality

Our premium stays — Namaste Dwaar Resort and IndRaj Villa — blend Indian tradition with luxury comfort, creating unforgettable experiences that celebrate our rich cultural heritage.

Namaste India Handicrafts

Celebrating India's artistry through handcrafted decor, furniture, and export products that showcase the timeless beauty and craftsmanship of our talented artisans worldwide.
